About
IndabaX 2024 IndabaX Tunisia is a local Deep Learning Indaba that brings together professionals and AI enthusiasts to exchange ideas, network, and learn about the latest developments in the Artificial Intelligence field. At this two-day event, attendees can expect to engage in keynote speeches, hands-on workshops, and a 20-hour hackathon. For its 5th edition, IEEE Sup'Com Student Branch is hosting this event to meet your expectations and offers a variety of opportunities to expand your network and deepen your understanding of the AI field.

Amal Rannen Triki

Research Scientist at Google DeepMind

Amal is a research scientist in the field of machine learning and artificial intelligence. Her research is driven by increasing accessibility to modern technologies and has been focused on improving neural networks efficiency in the large sense.
Her works cover data efficiency through regularisation and specialised objectives, computational efficiency leveraging continual and online learning, and efficient deployment with model compression. Amal is currently a senior research scientist at Google DeepMind.
She joined DeepMind in 2019. Since then, she has additionally been a member of the executive committee of the Deep Learning Indaba, joining forces with this amazing community to Strengthen African Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. She has also been an organiser of the Conference on Lifelong Learning Agents (CoLLAs) for the last two years.
Amal holds a PhD from the department of Electrical Engineering (ESAT) of KU Leuven (Belgium). Her PhD work was supervised by Prof. dr. Matthew B. Blaschko and partly conducted in Yonsei University (South Korea).
Before starting her research journey, she pursued engineering studies in CentraleSupelec (France) supported by a Tunisian state scholarship.
