About
IndabaX 2024 IndabaX Tunisia is a local Deep Learning Indaba that brings together professionals and AI enthusiasts to exchange ideas, network, and learn about the latest developments in the Artificial Intelligence field. At this two-day event, attendees can expect to engage in keynote speeches, hands-on workshops, and a 20-hour hackathon. For its 5th edition, IEEE Sup'Com Student Branch is hosting this event to meet your expectations and offers a variety of opportunities to expand your network and deepen your understanding of the AI field.

Meet Our Speakers

Firas Ben Hassen

Lead Data Scientist
at Allianz

Biography:

Firas is the Deputy Head of Data Science Services at Allianz Technology, where he leads his team towards innovative solutions. He also serves as the Program Manager for AllianzGPT, overseeing its development for the insurance and financial services sector.
Additionally, Firas is a respected guest AI lecturer at the Technical University of Munich, bridging academia with industry practice.
Firas's academic journey is as impressive as his professional endeavors. An alumnus of the Higher School of Communications of Tunis (Sup'Com) He holds a master's degree in AI from the University of Passau, Germany, furthering his expertise for his current role at Allianz.

Amal Rannen Triki

Research scientist
at DeepMind

Biography:

Amal, a research scientist in machine learning and artificial intelligence, focuses on enhancing accessibility to modern technologies. Her work at Google DeepMind emphasizes improving neural network efficiency through various methods, including data and computational efficiency, and model compression.
Amal is dedicated to strengthening African machine learning and AI through her involvement with the Deep Learning Indaba and organizing the Conference on Lifelong Learning Agents.
She holds a PhD from KU Leuven (Belgium) and has engineering studies background from CentraleSupelec (France) supported by a Tunisian state scholarship.
