About
IndabaX 2024 IndabaX Tunisia is a local Deep Learning Indaba that brings together professionals and AI enthusiasts to exchange ideas, network, and learn about the latest developments in the Artificial Intelligence field. At this two-day event, attendees can expect to engage in keynote speeches, hands-on workshops, and a 20-hour hackathon. For its 5th edition, IEEE Sup'Com Student Branch and InstaDeep are collaborating to deliver an event that meets your expectations and offers a variety of opportunities to expand your network and deepen your understanding of the AI field.

In partnership with
ZINDI

Zindi stands as Africa's pioneering data science competition platform. It serves as the epicenter for a comprehensive data science community, encompassing scientists, engineers, academics, companies, NGOs, governments, and institutions, all united in addressing Africa's most urgent challenges.

A 20-hour hackathon to put your skills to the test

This 20-hour hackathon, hosted on Zindi, W offers data scientists and engineers a chance to prove their skills by addressing real-world challenges. Participants will analyze datasets under time constraints to achieve their goals.
