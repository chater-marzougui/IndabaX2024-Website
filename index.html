IndabaX Tunisia 2024

Welcome to the 5th edition of IndabaX Tunisia! Dive into two action-packed days filled with panels, workshops, talks, hackathon, and more. This year, we're thrilled to introduce a poster session. AI enthusiasts, this is your moment! Don't miss out!

May 18th & 19th, 2024

Sup’com, Technopole El Ghazela

About
IndabaX 2024 IndabaX Tunisia is a local Deep Learning Indaba that brings together professionals and AI enthusiasts to exchange ideas, network, and learn about the latest developments in the field of Artificial Intelligence. At this two-day event, attendees can expect to engage in keynote speeches, hands-on workshops, and a 20-hour hackathon. For its 5th edition, the IEEE Sup'Com Student Branch is hosting this event to meet your expectations and offer a variety of opportunities to expand your network and deepen your understanding of the AI field.

Amal Rannen Triki

Research Scientist at Google DeepMind

Amal is a research scientist in the field of machine learning and artificial intelligence. Her research is driven by increasing accessibility to modern technologies and has been focused on improving neural networks efficiency in the large sense.
Her works cover data efficiency through regularisation and specialised objectives, computational efficiency leveraging continual and online learning, and efficient deployment with model compression. Amal is currently a senior research scientist at Google DeepMind.
She joined DeepMind in 2019. Since then, she has additionally been a member of the executive committee of the Deep Learning Indaba, joining forces with this amazing community to Strengthen African Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. She has also been an organiser of the Conference on Lifelong Learning Agents (CoLLAs) for the last two years.
Amal holds a PhD from the department of Electrical Engineering (ESAT) of KU Leuven (Belgium). Her PhD work was supervised by Prof. dr. Matthew B. Blaschko and partly conducted in Yonsei University (South Korea).
Before starting her research journey, she pursued engineering studies in CentraleSupelec (France) supported by a Tunisian state scholarship.

Amal Tarifa

Assistant Professor at ENSI

Dr. Amal Tarifa is an esteemed Assistant Professor at ENSI (National School of Computer Science), where she currently excels as a dedicated educator and researcher specializing in AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Natural Language Processing (NLP). With over 6 years of experience in IT instruction and over 9 years in the AI field, Dr. Amal Tarifa is recognized for her expertise and leadership in the realm of AI and data sciences. During her PhD, she played a pivotal role in developing an Agent-based application to evaluate personalization within web applications using various innovative algorithms. Before joining ENSI, Dr. Amal Tarifa was a Professor and Researcher at ESPRIT from 2018 to 2023. She pioneered active learning pedagogy and developed course materials covering SQL, data mining, BERT, GANs, and supervised engineering thesis projects on deep learning algorithms. She also collaborated internationally to develop tools for finance and healthcare sectors. Besides, she actively participated in prestigious events such as the NVIDIA GTC conference 2022-2024 as a member of the instructor team. Dr.Amal Tarifa is an NVIDIA ambassador. She is a member of the scientific community for many renowned journals and conferences. Dr. Amal Tarifa has also made significant industry contributions, having previously served as a Research Intern at HEUDIASYC-UTC-COMPIEGNE, where she developed machine learning algorithms for educational assessment, and as a Freelance researcher at TUNPIXEL, where she designed algorithms for recruitment processes. Her professional journey also includes a tenure as a Computer Programmer at DALEELTEQ, during which she designed and developed Spring based applications for the national export promotion centers. Her dedication to advancing the frontiers of AI through education, research, and industry collaboration underscores her status as a leading figure in the field of data science and artificial intelligence.
