IndabaX Tunisia 2024

Welcome to the 5th edition of IndabaX Tunisia! Dive into two action-packed days filled with panels , workshops, talks,hackathon and more. This year, we're thrilled to introduce a poster session. AI enthusiasts, this is your moment! Don't miss out

May 18th & 19th, 2024

Sup’com, Technopole El Ghazela

About
IndabaX 2024 IndabaX Tunisia is a local Deep Learning Indaba that brings together professionals and AI enthusiasts to exchange ideas, network, and learn about the latest developments in the Artificial Intelligence field. At this two-day event, attendees can expect to engage in keynote speeches, hands-on workshops, and a 20-hour hackathon. For its 5th edition, IEEE Sup'Com Student Branch and InstaDeep are collaborating to deliver an event that meets your expectations and offers a variety of opportunities to expand your network and deepen your understanding of the AI field.

In partnership with ZINDI

Zindi is the first data science competition platform in Africa. Zindi hosts an entire data science ecosystem of scientists, engineers, academics, companies, NGOs, governments and institutions focused on solving Africa’s most pressing problems.

20-hour hackathon for you to test your abilities

The 20-hour hackathon will focus on Biotechnologies and will be hosted on Zindi’s platform. It is a unique chance for data scientists and engineers to demonstrate their expertise to solve real world problems related to the intersection of biology and technology. Participants in the hackathon will be challenged to analyze datasets with a limited amount of time to complete the task.
